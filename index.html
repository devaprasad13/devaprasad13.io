Amazon Prime

Amazon Prime is a subscription service for online shoppers. While you don't have to be a member to buy things on Amazon, Prime gives you a wide range of benefits, like same-day shipping and grocery delivery. You can also take advantage of exclusive discounts and early lightning deals during sale events like Amazon Prime Day, which takes place on July 11 and 12 in 2023.

Disney

Disney+ Hotstar is an Indian subscription video on-demand over-the-top streaming service owned by The Walt Disney Company India and operated by Disney Streaming, Disney Entertainment both division Subsidiary of The Walt Disney Company, featuring domestic Indian film, television and sport content for India itself and its worldwide diaspora.Following the acquisition of Star India's company 21st Century Fox by Disney in 2019, Hotstar was integrated with the company's new global streaming brand Disney+ as 'Disney+ Hotstar' in April 2020.
